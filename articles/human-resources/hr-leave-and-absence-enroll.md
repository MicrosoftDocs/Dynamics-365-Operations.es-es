3. <span data-ttu-id="29106-108">Ajuste la siguiente configuración según corresponda:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="29106-108">Adjust the following settings as appropriate:</span></span>

   - <span data-ttu-id="29106-111">**Fecha de finalización**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="29106-111">**End date**</span></span>
   - <span data-ttu-id="29106-112">**Base de nivel**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="29106-112">**Tier basis**</span></span>
