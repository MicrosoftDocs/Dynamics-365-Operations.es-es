Esta plantilla sincroniza datos entre aplicaciones de Finance and Operations y Common Data Service.

---|---|---|---
NOMBRE | = | msdyn_paymentday.msdyn_name |

FRECUENCIA | >< | msdyn_frequency | 
DAYOFWEEK | >< | msdyn_dayofweek | 
DAYOFMONTH | = | msdyn_dayofmonth |
