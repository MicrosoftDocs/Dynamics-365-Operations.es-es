# <a name="accrue-subscription-revenue"></a><span data-ttu-id="223b2-103">Acumular ingresos de suscripción</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="223b2-103">Accrue subscription revenue</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="223b2-104">Haga clic en **Gestión de servicio** \> **Periódica** \> **Suscripciones de servicio** \> **Acumular ingresos de suscripción**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="223b2-104">Click **Service management** \> **Periodic** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **Accrue subscription revenue**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="223b2-107">En el campo **Fecha de registro**, especifique una fecha de registro y haga clic en **Aceptar**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="223b2-107">In the **Posting date** field, enter a posting date, and then click **OK**.</span></span>
