12. <span data-ttu-id="9dbbf-130">Cierre la página.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9dbbf-130">Close the page.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="9dbbf-131">En el panel de acciones, seleccione **Opciones**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9dbbf-131">On the Action Pane, select **Options**.</span></span>
