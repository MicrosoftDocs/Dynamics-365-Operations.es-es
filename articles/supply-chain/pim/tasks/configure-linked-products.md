# <a name="configure-linked-products"></a><span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-103">Configurar productos vinculados</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-103">Configure linked products</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-104">Este procedimiento muestra la configuración de productos vinculados.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-104">This procedure walks through configuring linked products.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-105">Este procedimiento usa los datos de demostración USRT y supone que ambos productos ya se han creado y liberado.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-105">This procedure uses the USRT demo data, and assumes both products have already been created and released.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-106">Vaya a Gestión de información de productos > Productos > Productos emitidos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-106">Go to Product information management > Products > Released products.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-107">En la lista, busque y seleccione el registro deseado.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-107">In the list, find and select the desired record.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-108">En el panel de acciones, haga clic en Retail y Commerce.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-108">On the Action Pane, click Retail and Commerce.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-109">Haga clic en Productos vinculados.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-109">Click Linked products.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-110">Haga clic en Editar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-110">Click Edit.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-111">En el campo Código de producto vinculado, haga clic en el botón desplegable para abrir la búsqueda.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-111">In the Linked product number field, click the drop-down button to open the lookup.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-112">En la lista, haga clic en el vínculo de la fila seleccionada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-112">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-113">En el campo Unidad, haga clic en el botón desplegable para abrir la búsqueda.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-113">In the Unit field, click the drop-down button to open the lookup.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-114">En la lista, haga clic en el vínculo de la fila seleccionada.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-114">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-115">En el campo Cantidad, especifique un número.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-115">In the Quantity field, enter a number.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="2a6c0-116">Haga clic en Guardar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a6c0-116">Click Save.</span></span>
