<span data-ttu-id="e747e-104">Este procedimiento muestra la configuración de productos vinculados.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e747e-104">This procedure walks through configuring linked products.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e747e-105">Este procedimiento usa los datos de demostración USRT y supone que ambos productos ya se han creado y liberado.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e747e-105">This procedure uses the USRT demo data, and assumes both products have already been created and released.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="e747e-110">Haga clic en Editar.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e747e-110">Click Edit.</span></span>
