## <a name="upload-a-video"></a><span data-ttu-id="40416-116">Subir un vídeo</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40416-116">Upload a video</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="40416-117">Para subir un vídeo al generador de sitios, siga estos pasos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40416-117">To upload a video in site builder, follow these steps.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="40416-118">En el panel de navegación izquierdo, seleccione **Biblioteca multimedia**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="40416-118">In the left navigation pane, select **Media Library**.</span></span>
