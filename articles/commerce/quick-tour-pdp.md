## <a name="recommendations-module"></a><span data-ttu-id="08980-126">Módulo de recomendaciones</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08980-126">Recommendations module</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="08980-130">Hay diferentes tipos de listas de recomendaciones disponibles:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08980-130">Different types of recommendation lists are available:</span></span>
