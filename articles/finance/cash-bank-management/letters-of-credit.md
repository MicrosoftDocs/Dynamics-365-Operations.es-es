# <a name="letters-of-credit"></a><span data-ttu-id="12af1-103">Carta de crédito</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-103">Letters of credit</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="12af1-104">Los créditos documentarios son documentos bancarios que se suelen usar para la compra y la venta de bienes entre fronteras internacionales.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-104">Letters of credit are bank documents that are commonly used for the purchase and sale of goods across international borders.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="12af1-105">Las cartas de crédito se usan en transacciones internacionales para garantizar que se efectuarán los pagos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-105">Letters of credit are used for international transactions to ensure that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="12af1-106">Una carta de crédito es un contrato que emite un banco, por el cual el banco garantiza el pago en nombre del comprador, siempre que se cumplan las condiciones del contrato entre el comprador y el vendedor.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-106">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to ensure payment on behalf of the buyer, if the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="12af1-107">Recuerde que una carta de crédito también se conoce como un crédito documentario (CD).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-107">Note that a letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="12af1-108">Para un crédito documentario de importación, la entidad jurídica es el comprador o el candidato para el crédito documentario.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-108">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="12af1-109">Para un crédito documentario de exportación, la entidad jurídica es el vendedor o el beneficiario del crédito documentario.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-109">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="12af1-110">Las partes siguientes están involucradas en una carta de crédito:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-110">The following parties are involved with a letter of credit:</span></span> 

 - <span data-ttu-id="12af1-111">El candidato (comprador) que pretende pagar la mercancía</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-111">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span> 
 - <span data-ttu-id="12af1-112">El beneficiario (vendedor) que recibirá el pago</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-112">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="12af1-113">El banco emisor que emite el crédito documentario</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-113">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="12af1-114">El banco avisador que realiza la transacción en nombre del candidato</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-114">The advising bank that carries out the transaction on behalf of the applicant</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="12af1-115">El crédito documentario incluye una descripción de las mercancías, los documentos requeridos, la fecha de envío y la fecha de vencimiento después de la que el pago no se realizará.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-115">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment will not be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="12af1-116">El banco emisor cobra un margen por el crédito documentario.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-116">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="12af1-117">Un crédito documentario puede ser revocable o irrevocable.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-117">A letter of credit can be revocable or irrevocable.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="12af1-118">La naturaleza de una carta de crédito puede ser transferible, no transferible o rotativa.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-118">The nature of a letter of credit can be transferable, non transferable, or revolving.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="12af1-119">Normalmente, un crédito documentario es un contrato irrevocable y confirmado que garantiza el pago a un beneficiario específico tras el envío de la documentación completa y precisa del envío.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="12af1-119">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>
