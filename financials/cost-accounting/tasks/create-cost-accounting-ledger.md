[!include[task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="64fb2-104">Un libro mayor de contabilidad de costes representa la unidad de notificación global.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64fb2-104">A cost accounting ledger represents the overall reporting unit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="64fb2-105">Se define por una dimensión de elemento de coste, la dimensión estadística, el calendario fiscal y la divisa.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64fb2-105">It is defined by a cost element dimension, statistical dimension, fiscal calendar, and currency.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="64fb2-106">Es agnóstico al concepto de entidades jurídicas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64fb2-106">It is agnostic to the concept of legal entities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="64fb2-107">Una entidad jurídica y sus datos se pueden asociar a varios libros mayores de contabilidad de costes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64fb2-107">A legal entity and its data can be associated with many cost accounting ledgers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="64fb2-108">Este registro usa la empresa USP2 con los datos para demostración.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64fb2-108">This recording uses the USP2 demo data company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="64fb2-109">Vaya a Contabilidad de costes > Configuración de libro mayor > Libros mayores de contabilidad de costes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64fb2-109">Go to Cost accounting > Ledger setup > Cost accounting ledgers.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="64fb2-111">En el campo Nombre, escriba un valor.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64fb2-111">In the Name field, type a value.</span></span>
